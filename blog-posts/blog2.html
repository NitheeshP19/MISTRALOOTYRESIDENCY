Living at 2,200 Metres

High-altitude living at MISTRAL OOTY Residency offers transformative benefits. Located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, our luxury mountain residences provide an escape from the ordinary.

The Benefits of Mountain Air

At 2,200 metres elevation, the air is crisp, clean, and invigorating. Our Ooty hill station properties offer natural climate control, with temperatures that remain pleasant year-round. This is elevated living in its truest sense.

The architecture of MISTRAL OOTY is designed to maximize these natural advantages. Floor-to-ceiling windows in every private suite ensure constant connection with the mountain environment.

Climate and Lifestyle

Living in the Western Ghats means experiencing nature's rhythms intimately. Morning mist, afternoon sun, evening clouds—each day brings new vistas. Our luxury residences are positioned to capture these ever-changing views.

The sustainable design of MISTRAL OOTY ensures comfort without compromising the environment. Local materials and thoughtful architecture create homes that breathe with the seasons.
